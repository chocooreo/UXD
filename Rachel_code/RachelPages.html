History of Chanens Room & Suites



Experience a curated collection of the finest entertainment, culture, cuisine, nightlife, and unforgettable experiences delivered right to your doorstep. Immerse yourself in the awe-inspiring splendor of the Chanens Building, a magnificent neoclassical masterpiece that has stood proudly since its construction in 2000. With its illustrious past spanning almost a century, the Chanens Building has played an integral role in shaping the captivating narrative of Singapore's vibrant history.

Today, the Chanens Hotel Singapore emerges as a captivating gem, having undergone a remarkable transformation into a breathtaking 500-room luxurious hotel. Imbued with timeless elegance and cultural significance, this enchanting establishment seamlessly blends the allure of the past with modern-day luxury. Discover a captivating of luxurious and modern intertwine to create an unforgettable experience for every guest who walks through its doors.
