-MERLION COVE-

Situated at the center of the esteemed Fullerton Building, Merlion Cove presents an enduring ambiance perfect for indulging in leisurely dining and afternoon tea.

Remaining faithful to the English tradition, Merlion Cove Traditional Afternoon Tea offers a delightful array of expertly crafted sandwiches and exquisite pastries. Accompanied by the enchanting melodies of a harpist, the high-ceiling atrium becomes a captivating setting on selected days.


Indulge in a delightful selection of time-honored teatime classics at Merlion Cove. Delight in the sophistication of the cucumber sandwich, a beloved favorite of Victorian-era aristocrats. Savor the distinctively English Battenberg Cake, originally crafted in 1884 as the royal wedding cake for Princess Victoria and Prince Louis of the Battenberg family, a dessert that epitomizes quintessential English indulgence.
